# Privacy Policy — 253874 快速转发 (Forum Clipper)

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

---

## 🚫 We Do Not Collect Anything

This browser extension does **NOT** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

| | |
|---|---|
| ❌ | We do not collect any personal information |
| ❌ | We do not track your browsing history |
| ❌ | We do not send any data to external servers |
| ❌ | We do not use cookies or tracking technologies |
| ❌ | We do not share any information with third parties |
| ❌ | We do not use analytics or telemetry |

## ✅ How the Extension Works

- **All processing is local** — Content extraction and formatting happen entirely in your browser
- **On-demand access** — Web page content is only accessed when you actively right-click and select the forward option
- **Targeted delivery** — Extracted content is sent **only to 253874.net forum** for the purpose of creating a post
- **Local settings** — Extension settings are stored in your browser (`chrome.storage.sync`)

## 📋 Data Flow

When you use the extension:

1. You right-click on a webpage and select "转发到 253874"
2. The extension extracts the page content **locally** (in your browser only)
3. The extracted content is sent to **253874.net** to fill in the post form
4. You review and submit the post yourself

> **No data ever leaves your computer except what you explicitly choose to post to 253874.net.**
